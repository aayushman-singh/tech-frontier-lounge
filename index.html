About the community

A low-key room for people who build cool things.

Tech Frontier Lounge is a monthly meetup for developers, designers and AI-curious folks in Delhi. No badges, no hard sell — just people who like making things, talking shop, and figuring out what's next.

Every edition mixes open hangout time with a couple of short talks from the community. Whether you're shipping in production or hacking on a weekend project, you'll find your people here.
